藻
zǎo

Meaning

algae, seaweed

Composition

Consists of the grass radical (艹) on top and 喿 below, indicating a type of plant that makes noise (rustling in the water)

Related Phrases

海藻 (hǎi zǎo) - seaweed
绿藻 (lǜ zǎo) - green algae
藻类 (zǎo lèi) - algae
藻井 (zǎo jǐng) - caisson (ceiling decoration)
水藻 (shuǐ zǎo) - aquatic algae
