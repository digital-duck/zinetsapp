藻
zǎo

Meaning

algae; seaweed

Composition

'艹' (grass radical) + '喿' (noise/chirping - phonetic component).

Related Phrases

海藻 (hǎi zǎo) - seaweed
藻类 (zǎo lèi) - algae
绿藻 (lǜ zǎo) - green algae
藻饰 (zǎo shì) - embellishment; ornamentation (literary)
蓝藻 (lán zǎo) - blue algae
